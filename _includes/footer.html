Find your genius

Compass is a learner-centered, design thinking education model guiding 6-12 graders along a journey to find their own genius. Employing creative problem-solving and real-world, true-grit-building experiences, kids leave Compass with a portfolio, a plan and a clear path to becoming amazing young adults and impactful world citizens.
